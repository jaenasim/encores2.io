The Plot of Encores

Crescent Afterhours Productions 2022

"You keep looking forward, trying to do more and more and more. But you forget to look behind too. At all the good you've already done."

The lives of three actresses collide at a second-rate production company specializing in Disney stage adaptations.

Clarisse has finally left the strict upbringing of her ambitious and very wealthy parents behind her. Rachelle's mum is the family's sole breadwinner, leaving Rachelle to pick up her little brother from daycare every day. It's a good thing Rachelle loves him so much. And Narella, a diva both on and off stage, struggles between work and love with her high school sweetheart of 4 years.

While their blossoming friendship see them through tough rehearsals, tougher personal problems begin to surface. As the real world becomes harder to navigate, they dive deeper and deeper into their Disney worlds. Can these three friends hang on long enough to keep each other afloat? Or will they lose everything they are?

Yet... is that really such a bad thing?
