NarellaLion King's Simba“A show? This is not a show, it’s her life!"
A character that makes jaws drop the moment she enters. A character with big emotions and a bigger attitude. She speaks her mind even as she struggles through balancing her desire to act and, well, other priorities. Narella is living her dream, but what happens when it all breaks down? Can she do anything more to fix things? Piece it back together?
RachelleMaleficent's MaleficentNo father? Check. Help her single mother raise a family? Check. Babysit her little brother? The list goes on.
Rachelle has spent her entire life putting everyone and everything before herself. No stomaches are left empty, no one left alone, not even an ounce of unhappiness. Still, those responsibilities come at a price long-due, as she struggles to balance everyone else's needs and her own. After being a hero her entire life, the time of villainy has descended upon her. But would it prove too much to bear? Of course not! It's Rachelle. She can take anything.
ClarisseBrave's Merida"A whole new world, a new fantastic point of view!"
Come along on Clarisse’s journey as she steps out of her sheltered environment into the real world. Raised as an heiress, Clarisse is finally pursuing her own dreams instead of her parents'. Out there in the great unknown, she will learn to face her emotions and discover that perhaps she cannot always control everything.
