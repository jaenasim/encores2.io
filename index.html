Encores is a student-written, directed and produced play by Crescent Afterhours 2022. The play is centred around the personal struggles of three characters.

Set in a modern second-rate theatre company, the play follows Clarisse, Narella and Rachelle as they grapple with the responsibilities and challenges they face in their lives.

In Encores, we explore what happens when life becomes too difficult to accept any longer, and we fall deeper and deeper into the masks we wear. With Disney movies as inspiration, the play both celebrates the Disney narrative of good trumping evil and challenges the convenience of Disney's 'Happily Ever Afters'.

Please sit back and enjoy Fantasmic, a Disneyland soundtrack played for Encores' Opening and Curtains Call music. And read on to find out more about the team that brought Encores to life, and about the play itself.
