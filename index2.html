About Afterhours

A Crescent Hall Cultural Interest Group

Crescent Afterhours is an interest group in Crescent Hall founded in 2016. Its Facebook account describes it as a "bunch of theatre-lobing, drama-mama friends".

And it certainly lives up to its name! Afterhours members are one bunch of crazy, ambitious and fun individuals, who bring together skills and experiences from various different walks of life. Afterhours is a melting pot of life, love and laughter.

Each year, a new Afterhours crew is formed, comprising of Directors, Producers, Stage Managers, Props, Box, Publicity, Financial Controllers, and Business Managers. These various subcommittees pull together to make the production a success year on year.
